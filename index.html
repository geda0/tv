Generative Video Platform

A browser-only creator studio: pick a 3D character, drop in a video, add TTS or voice-triggered cues, then record the canvas + mixed audio to a WebM file.

  1. Pick a character in the left panel.
  2. Optionally Load video in the studio bar.
  3. Click Init audio (grants mic + starts the mix).
  4. Add TTS cues or toggle Voice cues to capture speech.
  5. Start export, then Stop export, then Download WebM.

All processing is on-device. The TTS model downloads once on first use.
